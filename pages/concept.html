Concept

Combining the modern and the retro, the hotel's architecture and interior design reflect the characteristics of the local island elements, embracing the contemporary Western accents and timeless Eastern influences.

Listen To The Sound Of Bliss In Pure Silence

A Mediterranean Harmony Under The open Sky

Being in the heart of Sunrise Island, you will not hear the hustle and the bustle of the city center, thanks to multiple sound barriers between wall layers, creating a completely soundproof area that keeps out any extra noise generated inside and outside the villas.

In Sunrise Hotel, what you hear might be the sound of a water drop, the rustle of a leaf, the timber of the west wind, and a chandelier swaying around... A beautiful song or the voice of a loved one...
